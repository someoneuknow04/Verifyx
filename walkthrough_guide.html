Walkthrough – How to Verify Documents

Follow the steps below to easily verify each document or registration

Doctor Verification (NMC)

  1. Go to the NMC Portal.
  2. Select "Search by Name or Registration Number".
  3. Enter name or registration number, and choose the state council.
  4. Click "Search" to view registration details.

Lawyer Verification (State Bar Councils)

  1. Go to your respective state’s Bar Council portal. (e.g., Karnataka)
  2. Look for "Advocate Search" or "Roll of Advocates" section.
  3. Enter enrollment number or name to find advocate details.

Trade License Verification

  1. Visit your city’s municipal corporation portal. (e.g., Mumbai BMC)
  2. Go to the Trade License or Registration Services section.
  3. Enter application or license number to view status.
